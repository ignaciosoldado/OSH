# Occupational Risk Assessment Checklist 🛡️

This checklist is based on the **Occupational Risk Prevention Manual (CTRR0012)** for quick workplace inspections.

## 1. Signage and Environment
- [ ] Are emergency exits clearly marked (in green)?
- [ ] Are there prohibition signs in hazardous areas (in red)?
- [ ] Are walkways free of obstacles and loose cables?
- [ ] Is the lighting adequate for the task being performed?

## 2. Protection and Equipment (PPE)
- [ ] Do workers use the necessary protective equipment (helmets, gloves, etc.)?
- [ ] Is work equipment in good condition and CE-marked?
- [ ] Are there signs indicating the mandatory use of PPE (in blue)?

## 3. Ergonomics and Health
- [ ] Does the workstation setup allow for a neutral posture (back and neck)?
- [ ] Are rest breaks observed to prevent visual/physical fatigue?
- [ ] Is lifting performed safely (with bent knees)?

## 4. Emergencies (First Aid)
- [ ] Is the first-aid kit accessible and stocked with basic supplies (bandages, antiseptics)?
- [ ] Are fire extinguishers visible and at the proper pressure?
- [ ] Do employees know what to do in the event of an accident (**P**rotect, **A**lert, and **R**espond)?
